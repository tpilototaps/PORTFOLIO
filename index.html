ABOUT ME AND MY TECH INTERESTS

I have a keen interest in technology and software. I currently work in aeronautics as a UAV test Pilot and Flight Instructor. We use interesting software to operate and process UAV data postflight. The symbiosis between humankind and machine never ceases to inspire me. I find it very empowering to be able to enhance human capability by complementing it with computers to aid creativity and goal achievement through applications. I love to create applications using frameworks such as ReactJS and Flask as I really enjoy programming and writing code while tapping into my creativity. I enjoy being presented with a challenge and coming up with solutions to that challenge. To me, coding is exactly that from its fundamental core to its grander scale. It is very rewarding to be able to create something from nothing. The act of breaking down big tasks or problems into smaller, more manageable ones that I can then tackle in code to find a working solution to the bigger task is rewarding.

Building something beautiful and visually appealing such as an application that people can use that improves lives or helps achieve a particular goal is very exciting. I am a creative and artsy person hence my love for front-end development and creating beautiful applications. Although I am self-driven, I enjoy collaborating with people as I am a people person and I truly believe the adage that says 'We is greater than me'. The best achievements by mankind were achieved through team work. We can leverage the strengths of each other in a team to create a more powerful entity that is unstoppable.

Skilled in various disciplines that proove helpful in software development

My skills as a software developer are complimented by many skills gained through my years-long experience in manned and unmanned aeronautics. The skills I have gained through my professional career are crew resource management, situational awareness, attention to detail, team work while working with crews, and good communication skills.

Fantastic team player

My background working with unmanned aircraft systems has given me experience working with teams in which the members are either intra or interdepartmental. I have witnessed the incredible achievements made through team work and have experienced first hand what constitutes successful collaboration to produce a great product.

Excellent troubleshooter

My professional career in which I have worked with autonomous systems on unmanned aircraft and the computer components and wiring needed to drive those processes, has given me experience with troubleshooting. Being able to refer to documentation, use educated guesses, use the processes of elimination, and having an organized approach to problem solving is what makes great troubleshooting. Troubleshooting is and always will be part of working with tech.
